Our Profile

About Compatto

Compatto was founded in 2010 with a simple vision: to make premium furniture accessible and the furnishing process effortless for everyone. Over the years, we have grown from a small local business to a leading furniture solutions provider in Greece and Cyprus.

Our team consists of passionate interior designers, logistics experts, and customer service professionals who work together to transform your vision into reality. We believe that every space tells a story, and we're here to help you tell yours beautifully.

Our Team

Our Vision

To be the leading furniture solutions provider in the Mediterranean region, transforming the way people experience and interact with their living spaces.

We envision a world where beautiful, functional furniture is accessible to everyone, and where the process of furnishing your space is as enjoyable as living in it.
